The FHIR Advantage

The FHIR standard is the foundation of modern interoperability. Unlike proprietary silos that restrict data flow, FHIR is international and open. We leverage agentic coding to transform clinical data into actionable operational metrics.

Featured Tool: SUDS (Simple UDS)

SUDS is a free, open-source ETL and dashboarding engine designed for FQHCs. It automates the extraction and transformation of clinical data to simplify annual UDS reporting.

Powered by the Herding Cats framework—an agentic dbt platform for autonomous data pipelines with strict quality guardrails.
