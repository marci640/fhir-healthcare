SUDS: Simple UDS Reporting

Reducing the administrative tax on community health centers.

Why SUDS?

FQHCs are navigating a tightening fiscal landscape. Recent federal Medicaid redeterminations and funding cuts mean manual reporting is a risk to clinical operations. SUDS automates UDS submissions so centers can focus on patient care.

Unified Data Models

SUDS integrates UDS Financial Tables with clinical and demographic data, providing a holistic view of center performance proprietary silos cannot match.

UDS+ & FHIR Ready

Prepare for patient-level reporting. SUDS generates FHIR exports with a visualization layer to validate accuracy before submission.

Data Sovereignty

Own your metrics. Using open-source CQL engines, SUDS puts HEDIS digital quality measurements directly in the hands of the organization.

Built on eClinicalWorks & Tuva

Optimized for eClinicalWorks (eCW), SUDS leverages the open-source Tuva Connector to map raw clinical data into actionable UDS schemas instantly.
